Description

Effective date is a date at which the transfer has been processed or cancelled. When we validated a picking (Receive or Delivery Order), current today's date will always be chosen as it's processed date and that will also affect stock journals calculation.
But, what if you want to choose another previous past date instead?

Well, this is the exact module you need!

Features

Update Sales Order & Purchase Order

Change effective date on sales order & purchase order.

Update Stock Moves

The stock moves will change it's date as same as the effective date you pick.

Update & Rename Journal Entry

Not only change it's date, but also renaming the journal entry based on which date you pick.

Support Foreign Currency

Will automatically change the currency rate based on exchange rate of selected backdate day.

The Stock Valuation Will Also Follow...

If you set automated stock valuation, it will also follow the new date you choose.

Best Suits For Your Late Document Input

If you have the past-document or record that you need to work on today, this module will adjust the picking-related to the past-date.

Extra Privilege for Certain User

Give the privilege to certain User or Employee to re-pick the changed effective date.
